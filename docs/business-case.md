# Business Case

## Objective

A production-oriented RAG blueprint for governed, measurable, enterprise knowledge retrieval.

## Business problem

Enterprises are moving documents, policies, service knowledge, and operating procedures into GenAI workflows, but many RAG systems fail because they cannot prove accuracy, cite sources, or detect regressions.

## Target stakeholders

- CIO, CDO, CTO, and AI transformation leaders
- Data science and GenAI delivery teams
- Product owners and business operations leaders
- Risk, compliance, and governance teams

## Value hypothesis

- Faster movement from AI prototype to production-ready solution
- Better visibility into business value, risk, and reliability
- Reusable delivery assets for enterprise AI programs
- Stronger alignment between architecture decisions and measurable outcomes

## Success measures

- Reduction in manual cycle time
- Measurable quality, latency, cost, or adoption improvement
- Clear governance checkpoints before scale-up
- Repeatable patterns reusable across multiple business domains
